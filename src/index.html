About LOdown

We're happy to be hosting our 2nd annual LOdown LIVE! This conference was created by the Student Solidarity Committee. This Committee works towards social action awareness and education, inclusion and anti-racism and equity at Lillian Osborne High School. With the support of the Parent Council and staff we were able create a space for speakers to share innovative ideas to help to inspire and engage the community in conversations. We hope to bring together bright minds to give focused talks on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning and provoke conversation that matter. Our main theme this year is Amelioration: Perspectives on Self Improvement.

About the SSC

The Student Solidarity Committee (SSC) acknowledges the struggles of the students staff in our school, so our mission is to harness the social justice passion in our students and turn it into concrete actions to help both individuals and groups, especially those who belong to minorities, in our local community.

FAQ

What is LOdown LIVE?
LOdown LIVE is our school’s annual conference event, where we seek to elevate youth voices by providing a platform for students to speak and learn from esteemed community members. We invite speakers from across Alberta to share their experience.
Will there be food provided at the event?
LOdown LIVE provides a variety of snacks, lunch, and dinner from various places. LOdown LIVE is committed to providing a space for individuals to network and gain more knowledge and experience from others over the medium of food. Our dinner will take place at 5:30 P.M and will be provided for everyone.

How was the theme chosen for this year?
Lillian Osborne is home to many aspiring students brimming with initiative and drive. From student associations cultivating a community, to STEM researchers making an impact in the scientific field for the betterment of society, everyone here is consistently improving themselves to fit the vision that they want to create, and that's something unique to Osborne -- something you can't find anywhere else. We chose the theme to foster the drive and leadership home at Lillian Osborne to the next level.
How does LOdown LIVE aim to elevate youth voices through its platform?
LOdown LIVE aims to have people from numerous backgrounds with different experiences share their ideas regarding self-improvement. Many of our speakers are students from our schools or youth in our community who strive to share their everyday experiences. We aim to foster their voice through LOdown LIVE.
